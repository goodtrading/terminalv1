----------------------------------------
1. Add VACUUM PROXIMITY logic
----------------------------------------

Create a new metric:

vacuumProximity

Purpose:
Measure how close current price is to entering a thin-liquidity zone that could trigger acceleration.

Inputs:
- current orderbook depth by price zone
- nearby resting liquidity
- spread behavior
- short-term price velocity
- distance to nearest thin-liquidity area

Output:
A normalized score from 0 to 100.

Interpretation:
0–25   = LOW
26–50  = MEDIUM
51–75  = HIGH
76–100 = IMMINENT

Render in Liquidity Map panel as:

VACUUM PROXIMITY
LOW / MEDIUM / HIGH / IMMINENT
