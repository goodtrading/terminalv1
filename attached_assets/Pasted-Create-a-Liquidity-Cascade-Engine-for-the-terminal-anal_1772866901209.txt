Create a Liquidity Cascade Engine for the terminal analytics system.

Goal:
Detect potential liquidation cascades and identify the price levels that could trigger accelerated market moves.

Use existing analytics already available in the terminal.

Inputs available:

spot price
gammaCliffs
gammaSlope
gammaRegimeBand
dealerSensitivity
cascadeRisk
expansionProbability
expansionDirection
gammaMagnets
callWall
putWall
trapZones
OI concentration
currentPlaybook
institutionalBias
tradeDirection

Compute the following outputs:

1. cascadeRisk

Return:

LOW
MEDIUM
HIGH
EXTREME

Rules:
- HIGH or EXTREME when short gamma conditions exist with nearby gamma cliffs and elevated dealer sensitivity
- MEDIUM when mixed signals exist
- LOW when long gamma compression dominates

2. cascadeDirection

Return:

UP
DOWN
TWO_SIDED
NONE

Rules:
- DOWN when bearish expansion signals dominate
- UP when bullish expansion signals dominate
- TWO_SIDED when volatility expansion exists in transition regimes
- NONE when compression dominates

3. cascadeTrigger

Return a short text describing the trigger level.

Examples:

"Break below dealer pivot"
"Break above call wall"
"Loss of gamma magnet support"

Use the nearest structural level relative to spot.

4. liquidationPocket

Return a price range where the cascade could accelerate.

Examples:

"64.5k – 65.5k"
"71k – 72k"

Prefer ranges near gamma cliffs or OI clusters.

5. cascadeDrivers

Return 3–5 short bullet-style explanations.

Example:

[
"Short gamma regime",
"Gamma cliff below spot",
"Dealer sensitivity elevated",
"Expansion probability rising"
]

Integration:

Add these fields to:

/api/options/summary
/api/terminal/state

Fields to add:

cascadeRisk
cascadeDirection
cascadeTrigger
liquidationPocket
cascadeDrivers

Do not remove any existing analytics.
Do not modify existing engines.
Only extend the analytics pipeline and expose the new fields to terminal state.
