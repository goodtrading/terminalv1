UI IMPROVEMENT — MARKET MODE INDICATOR + SIDEBAR CLARITY

Goal:
Make the trading terminal easier to interpret for non-expert traders by introducing a high-level market summary and improving visual hierarchy.

Important:
Do NOT change backend calculations or analytics engines.
Only improve the UI presentation.

------------------------------------------------

STEP 1 — ADD MARKET MODE INDICATOR

Create a new panel at the top of the right sidebar called:

MARKET MODE

This panel summarizes the current market environment derived from the existing analytics engines.

Example layout:

MARKET MODE
RANGE CONTROL

Possible values:

RANGE CONTROL
VOLATILITY EXPANSION
SQUEEZE RISK
TRANSITION

Styling:

• Larger font than other panels
• Center aligned
• Color-coded background or badge

Color rules:

RANGE CONTROL → blue
VOLATILITY EXPANSION → orange
SQUEEZE RISK → red
TRANSITION → yellow

This should visually stand out as the first thing traders read.
