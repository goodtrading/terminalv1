Create a Liquidity Sweep Detector v1 for the terminal analytics system.

Goal:
Detect likely liquidity sweeps where price may move into or through visible order book liquidity zones with acceleration.

Use existing analytics already available in the system.

Inputs available:
- spot price
- liquidityHeatZones
- liquidityConfluenceZones
- liquidityPressure
- marketMode
- marketModeConfidence
- hedgingFlowDirection
- hedgingFlowStrength
- hedgingAccelerationRisk
- cascadeRisk
- cascadeDirection
- squeezeProbability
- squeezeDirection
- institutionalBias
- tradeDirection
- dealerPivot
- gammaMagnets
- callWall
- putWall

Compute the following outputs:

1. sweepRisk

Return one of:
- LOW
- MEDIUM
- HIGH
- EXTREME

Rules:
- Increase risk when visible liquidity is close to spot and directional conditions align
- Increase risk when cascade or squeeze conditions support the move
- Increase risk when dealer hedging flow may amplify the move
- Reduce risk when price is balanced and long gamma stabilization dominates

2. sweepDirection

Return one of:
- UP
- DOWN
- TWO_SIDED
- NONE

Rules:
- UP when near ask liquidity and upside directional conditions dominate
- DOWN when near bid liquidity and downside directional conditions dominate
- TWO_SIDED when liquidity exists on both sides and regime is unstable
- NONE when sweep risk is low

3. sweepTrigger

Return a short readable trigger.

Examples:
- "Break above near ask liquidity"
- "Loss of bid support below pivot"
- "Reclaim dealer pivot into upside sweep"

4. sweepTargetZone

Return a short price range where the liquidity sweep is most likely to travel.

Examples:
- "68.1k – 68.4k"
- "67.0k – 66.6k"

Prefer the strongest nearby liquidity cluster in the sweep direction.

5. sweepSummary

Return 3–5 short readable bullet-style strings.

Examples:
[
  "Ask liquidity stacked above spot",
  "Hedging flow may support upside",
  "Sweep likely if price reclaims 68k"
]

Integration:

Add these fields to:
- /api/options/summary
- /api/terminal/state

Fields to add:
- sweepRisk
- sweepDirection
- sweepTrigger
- sweepTargetZone
- sweepSummary

Do not modify existing engines.
Do not redesign the UI yet.
Only extend the analytics pipeline.
