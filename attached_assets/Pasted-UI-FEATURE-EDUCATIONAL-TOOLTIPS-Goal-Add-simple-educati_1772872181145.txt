UI FEATURE — EDUCATIONAL TOOLTIPS

Goal:
Add simple educational tooltips that appear when users hover over key trading concepts.

This is designed to help beginner traders understand the terminal.

Do NOT change backend logic or calculations.
Only add UI tooltips.

------------------------------------------------

STEP 1 — ADD TOOLTIP SYSTEM

Add hover tooltips to the following elements:

Gamma Regime
Gamma Flip
Call Wall
Put Wall
Magnet
Dealer Pivot
Squeeze Risk
Transition Zone

------------------------------------------------

STEP 2 — TOOLTIP STYLE

Tooltips should be:

• small
• dark themed
• readable
• short educational text

Position:
near the hovered element

------------------------------------------------

STEP 3 — TOOLTIP CONTENT

Gamma Regime

LONG GAMMA
Dealers hedge against price moves.
This usually stabilizes price and creates mean reversion.

------------------------------------------------

Gamma Flip

GAMMA FLIP
Level where dealer positioning changes from stabilizing
the market to amplifying volatility.

------------------------------------------------

Call Wall

CALL WALL
Large concentration of call options.
Often acts as resistance due to dealer hedging.

------------------------------------------------

Put Wall

PUT WALL
Large concentration of put options.
Often acts as support due to dealer hedging.

------------------------------------------------

Magnet

GAMMA MAGNET
Strike with heavy options positioning.
Price often gravitates toward these levels.

------------------------------------------------

Dealer Pivot

DEALER PIVOT
Key level where dealer hedging flow may shift direction.

------------------------------------------------

Squeeze Risk

SQUEEZE RISK
Market conditions where hedging flows may accelerate
price movement rapidly.

------------------------------------------------

Transition Zone

TRANSITION ZONE
Area where the market moves between long gamma
and short gamma regimes.

------------------------------------------------

STEP 4 — UX RULES

Tooltips must:

• appear instantly on hover
• disappear when mouse leaves
• not block the chart
• remain compact

------------------------------------------------

Expected result:

Beginner users can hover any concept
and instantly understand what it means.
