Only improve the calculation engine.

Use the parsed options dataset to compute the following institutional metrics:

1. Total Gamma Exposure (GEX)
Formula:
GEX = gamma * open_interest * spot^2

Aggregate across all strikes.

2. Gamma Regime
Determine whether the market is:

LONG GAMMA
SHORT GAMMA

based on the sign of total GEX.

3. Gamma Flip
Find the price level where the cumulative gamma exposure changes sign.

Do not use a fixed value.
Compute it dynamically from the strike distribution.

4. Distance to Flip
Distance between spot and gamma flip:

abs(spot - gamma_flip) / spot * 100

5. Transition Zone
Create a dynamic band around the gamma flip.

Default:
±0.5% of spot.

6. Call Wall
Strike with the largest CALL open interest.

7. Put Wall
Strike with the largest PUT open interest.

8. Open Interest Concentration
Strike with the highest total open interest.

9. Dealer Pivot
Compute a weighted pivot strike using:

gamma * open_interest

This should represent the dealer hedging center.

10. Gamma Magnets
Identify the top 3 strikes with the highest positive GEX.

Sort by absolute gamma exposure.

11. Short Gamma Pocket
Detect the nearest contiguous region where net gamma is negative.

12. Deep Risk Pocket
Detect the next major negative gamma cluster farther from spot.

13. Gamma Acceleration
Measure how fast gamma changes around spot.

Classify as:

LOW
MODERATE
HIGH
EXTREME
